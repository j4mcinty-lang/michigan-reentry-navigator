Michigan Reentry Navigator is a free SMS-based resource navigation service for people recently released from incarceration in Michigan.

Service description

This service provides information and referrals to community resources including housing, identification documents, benefits, food assistance, and treatment services in Michigan. It is a resource navigator only and does not provide legal, medical, or clinical advice.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your carrier plan.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interaction with the service.

How to opt out

Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages. Reply START to resume.

How to get help

Reply HELP at any time for support information.

Limitations

This service is not a substitute for professional legal, medical, or mental health services. In case of emergency, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.

Contact

For questions reply HELP to any message.
