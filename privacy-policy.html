Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Michigan Reentry Navigator is a free SMS-based resource navigation service for people recently released from incarceration in Michigan.

What we collect

We receive your phone number and the content of messages you send to our service. We do not collect your name, location, or any other personal information unless you choose to share it in your messages.

How we use your information

Your phone number and messages are used solely to provide resource navigation responses. We do not sell, share, or distribute your information to any third parties.

Data retention

Conversation history is held temporarily in memory to maintain context during your session and is not permanently stored.

Opting out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, reply HELP to any message.
